COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Do I need to be fit or have experience in combat sports to be OlecraFit?

No you dont. At OlecraFit, we pride ourselves on our mission that everyone can be fit and feel great in their very own bodies. Our services fully cater to any able-bodied person and we will work with you step-by-step along the way to achieve your goals, while accomplishing our mission. That being said, if you have previous injuries / current injuries that can affect your training, we stress that you declare that to us on your profile details, enquiry form or when we are in contact with you because your health is the TOP PRIORITY in our training services.

Should I go with individual sessions or the training packages?

Buying our training packages will help you save up to 15% in the long run! If you are new to our services, we recommend that you start off with our limited one-time 10% off Olecra Intro Session before buying a package so you know you are comfortable with our services. If you are interested in both Training and Nutrition Services, we will recommend the Olecra Starters Package so you can save while taking steps to be healthier. Check out our shop here.

What are your opening times?

We do not have set opening times as our everyday schedule is based on the existing clientbase at that time. In order to cater to all our client's diverse schedules, We have partnered with a 24/7 gym, Platinum Fitness Singapore, to ensure quality services, facilities, equipment, convenient and centralised location for you.

Payment

In order to protect our scheduling and planning costs, we will require a 50% confirmation deposit to be made at least 48hours before your session. To provide payment transparency, we recommend PayNow or a Bank Transfer

I have some special circumstances regarding my fitness / health / schedule etc and am not sure if this is the right service for me

We acknowledge that everyone has their individual concerns and cirucumstances, so just send us an enquiry form here telling us your concerns and we will respond as soon as we can to resolve your uncertainties.

I don't have my own gear.

We provide all the equipment you need to have a proper session with us as well as fresh towels for use at the shower facilties provided. All you need is your water bottle, sports attire (wear shorts above knee length), and spare clothes.

Do you sell muay thai / boxing equipment?

We are not an apparel retailer and not a distributor. However, we want to highlight the importance of investing in your own gear and purchasing equipment of appropriate quality. We can recommend certain products that we have good experience with and for your convenience, help you purchase them at a small fee.

Last minute Postponements / Cancellations

Please postpone / cancel your session at least 48 hours in advance so we can avoid inconveniences to our other clients. We also understand that emergencies occur and will evaluate last minute cancellations on a case-by-case basis. While we try to as reasonable as possible, we will reserve the right not to provide any refund for the session's deposit if notice was given less than 48hours before the session.
