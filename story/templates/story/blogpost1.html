Muay Thai as Functional Fitness

Anyone can learn Muay Thai, let us show you how...

Human beings have evolved over millenniums to be able to move constantly on our feet, and recent technological advancements are leaving our physical bodies behind. We are witnessing an evolving modernised society being increasingly affiliated to a sedentary lifestyle. We are either on an electronic device at home or seated at desks at work. All this physical inactivity is not natural for our anatomy and comes at the cost of the two most important things to us, our health and well-being.

Meanwhile, large-scale fitness marketing are portraying "impossible-to-meet" fitness standards to the public and obesity rates rise year on year in Singapore and other developed nations.

At OlecraFit, we believe in functional fitness which utilises all your muscles at the same time while staying PRACTICAL and APPLICABLE in real-life situations.

We want to debunk the myth that fitness is a ripped body or having chunks of muscle. We believe that Fitness is you being a healthy individual feeling strong in your own body!

At OlecraFit, we utilise Muay Thai Techniques as that missing piece to give you health, fitness and intangible yet valuable gains like humility, self-confidence, mental toughness, self-defense skills, improved gait, self-discipline, respect, confidence, and so much more.

We recognise that most people might see Muay Thai as a sport of violence that requires high levels of fitness. We argue that ANYONE can pick up Muay Thai and we back it up with our 100% PERSONALISED Olecra sessions where we impart authentic Muay Thai technique to you while helping you achieve your health and fitness goals.

Start thinking more about your health and well-being today and join us to take the steps necessary to lead a comfortable and healthy life!
