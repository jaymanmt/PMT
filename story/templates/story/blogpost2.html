The Importance of Nutrition

The food you eat determines how you feel...

Your well-being is of the utmost importance, and there are some aspects of your health that even tremendous amounts of exercises will not improve unless you make the dietary changes that need to happen.

The lifestyles of Singapore Residents and the urbanised culture of the city-state make it difficult to understand the importance of nutrition and healthy eating. Many grow up not understanding the impacts of food on our bodies and what healthy eating actually means.

The situation does not get easier with fad diets and false web content spreading through social media catching so much attention. Such content and information are unfortunately often part of a marketing strategy and are not based on unbiased scientific research consolidated by qualified nutrition researchers.

At OlecraFit, we strive to change this around by providing an affordable nutrition consultation by a qualified nutritionist who we will work with you to educate and improve your health based on scientifically proven data as well as providing the essential nutrition advice needed to eliminate fad diets and discretionary food.

We will help you understand that whatever goes into your body will affect your health and quality of life. Take back control of what you eat for your own sake. Good Nutrition only begins with you.
